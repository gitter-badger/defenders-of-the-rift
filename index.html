Defenders of the Rift

Defenders of the Rift is an open-source Customizable Card Game (CCG). It has many of the elements you're used to finding in your favorite commercial CCG or TCG, but it has a couple distinct differences:

It's free and open

Defenders of the Rift is free to play and free to modify. And to that end...

You're on the team!

Defenders of the Rift is a collaborative effort. So get involved!

Card Calculator

The Card Calculator application is currently under development. The purpose of it is to balance card stats and resources, as this can be a very complex fomulation for game balance.
